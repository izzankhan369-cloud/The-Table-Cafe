Our Story

Brewed with Intention,
Served with Heart

The Garden Table was born from a simple belief: that a good cup of coffee and a warm plate of food can make any morning feel meaningful. Chef Priya Nair spent seven years honing her craft in Melbourne before returning home to build a café that felt like an extension of her own kitchen.

Every item on our menu is seasonal, thoughtfully sourced, and made fresh daily. We believe in slowing down, sitting with a friend, and savouring the small rituals that make life good.
